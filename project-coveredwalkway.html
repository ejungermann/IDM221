The covered walkway

Bartram Gardens, Philadelphia, PA

Winter 2022

9 weeks

This project adds program and adjusts the landscape of Bartram Gardens - an existing historical home and gardens open to the public. The new buildings/programs include greenhouses, an exhibition and classroom space, lab, an inn, event space, and a boathouse with a new dock. These new programs, which aim to further engage the community with the gardens, are connected by a continuous, covered walkway that loosely follows the topography to stay a consistant level. Inspired by the Japanese engawa, the walkway serves as a transitional space that connects the people to nature in all weather. The North half sits semi-underground and transitions to the Southern half that sits above ground with the preserved historic core in the center. With a variety of nature experiences - open meadow, dense forest, green field, and wetlands - a guest is able to immerse themselves in each one alone the walkway.
